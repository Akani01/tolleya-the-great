Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center for Vhembe West, Elim Circuit. Find answers to common questions and guidance for using our platform effectively.

Login Help

If you’re experiencing issues with logging in or navigating the platform, here’s how the system works based on your role:

  • Administrator Login: Administrators are redirected to the Admin Dashboard where they can manage the entire system, including users and settings.
  • Staff Login: Staff members are redirected to the Staff Dashboard, where they can manage school operations and track activities.
  • Student Login: Students are redirected to the Student Dashboard, where they can access assignments, grades, and resources.
  • Principal Login: Principals are directed to the Principal Dashboard, which provides oversight of school performance and staff.
  • Educator Login: Educators are redirected to the Educator Dashboard, where they can manage their classes, attendance, and reports.
  • Circuit Manager Login: Circuit Managers access the Circuit Manager Dashboard, providing tools to oversee multiple schools.
  • Parent Login: Parents are redirected to the Parent Dashboard, which helps them monitor their children’s academic progress and attendance.
  • Member Login: Members are redirected to the Member Dashboard, tailored to their specific roles within the organization.

If your login details are invalid, you will receive an error message. Please double-check your email and password or contact support if you need assistance.

Common Issues and Solutions

  • Forgot Password?

    Click on the Forgot Password link on the login page to reset your password.

  • Access Denied?

    Ensure that you are using the correct credentials and logging in with the email and password registered for your role.

  • Role-Based Issues?

    If you believe your role is incorrectly assigned, contact the system administrator for corrections.
