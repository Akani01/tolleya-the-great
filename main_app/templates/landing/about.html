Our Vision

Quality education for every child in every classroom in every school across the Elim Circuit.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, inclusive, and sustainable education solutions, ensuring academic excellence and holistic development.

Our History

The Elim Circuit was established to foster collaboration and innovation among schools in the region. With a legacy of over 20 years, we have grown to support 24 schools, impacting the lives of thousands of students and educators.
